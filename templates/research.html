Is Bitcoin Digital Gold?

Although bitcoin is known as a "cryptocurrency", this name is a little misleading for many people. Bitcoin can be used as a currency, the coins are able to be sent and transfered, however most experts agree that bitcoin is more compareable to gold rather than the US dollar or any other currency. This stems from the fact that bitcoin is a great store of value, and there are a couple of reasons for this.

Why does bitcoin have value?

  • Limited Supply
  • Decentralized
  • Borderless
  • Easily Sent and Recieved
  • Trustless and Safe
